About Me

In the past 15 years, I have had the opportunity to experience every side of the equation in software development. I have been the frustrated user just learning. I have been the functional business leader working to develop key enhancements. I have worked closely with IT, and I understand development cycles and the risks. Most importantly, I didn’t just implement projects and then take on a new role. I continued to work with the functional teams and got direct feedback on our solutions. I am very conscious of the customer experience. Transitions to new systems can be tough even in the best circumstances, so I work pro-actively to provide the best communication and training. I really enjoy driving quality process improvement, and I would love to be a part of an organization that is helping improve the world.
